Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nexus really free?

Yes! Nexus is and will always be free for all users. We believe AI tools should be accessible without barriers.

Are there any usage limits?

No – you can send as many requests as you like. The only limit is what your Groq account allows (free tier has rate limits, but that's on Groq's side).

Do I need to provide a credit card?

Not at all. Just sign in with Google and you're good to go.

What about my Groq API key – is it safe?

Absolutely. Your Groq key is encrypted and stored securely in Firestore. It never leaves our backend, and we use a separate Nexus key for the frontend widget.

Can I use it on multiple websites?

Yes – you can authorise up to 10 domains per account. Each domain can have its own widget instance.
