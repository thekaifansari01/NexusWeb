Why choose Nexus?

Everything you need to add AI to your site, built with security at its core.

Vault-Grade Security

Your Groq API key is encrypted and stored safely. We issue a separate, secure Nexus Key for your frontend widget, ensuring your billing is never compromised.

Strict Whitelisting

Our backend verifies the origin of every request. Nobody can steal your Nexus Key and use it on unauthorized domains. Total control over where your AI lives.

Zero Friction

Drop a single script tag into your HTML. The widget automatically adapts to your site's theme, reads your page content, and provides context-aware answers instantly.
